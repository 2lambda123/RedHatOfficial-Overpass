Overpass is the typeface used by Red Hat

Available for free, open source under the OFL+LGPL.

The origin-artwork that inspired Overpass (Highway Gothic) is public domain.

Used globally in road signage, Highway Gothic embodies design for the public good.

Standard roads and highway signage demonstrate that open standards and open communication help complex communities work better together.

This digital interpretation of Highway Gothic attempts to give modern designers the flexibility they expect for achieving modern typography online

Today’s enterprise software brands have their own distinct typographic identities. This means Red Hat could contribute Overpass to the public without confusing the market it operates in.

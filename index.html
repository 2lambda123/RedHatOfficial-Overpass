Overpass is the typeface used by the Red Hat Brand. All font files in this project are open source under the OFL and LGPL. Overpass is free to use and remix.

The origin-artwork that inspired Overpass (Highway Gothic) is part of the public domain.
Used globally in road signage, Highway Gothic characterises design for the public good.

Like our roads and highways, open standards and open communication mean complex communities can work better together.

Why is overpass free? Most enterprise software brands have their own distinct typographic language. For Red Hat, differentiation is less a concern than empowering you with this contribution.

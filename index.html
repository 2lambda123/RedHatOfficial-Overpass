Overpass is the font Red Hat uses to distinguish and brand itself as an enterprise software company. All fonts in Overpass are open source and available to everyone.

The source artwork that inspired Overpass (Highway Gothic) is part of the public domain. Used globally in public road signage, Highway Gothic is helpful, informative and characterises design for the public good.

This embodies the message Red Hat wants to convey: With the help of open standards, we can work together and get where we want to go.

Why is overpass free ? Because we want out voice to carry futher than the cubicles of our corporate design team. Other enterprise software brands have their own distinct typography so differentiation in the market is not a concern .
